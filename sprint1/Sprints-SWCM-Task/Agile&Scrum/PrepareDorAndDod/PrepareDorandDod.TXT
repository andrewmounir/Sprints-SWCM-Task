Defenition of Ready (DOR)

shared understanding with the scrum team of what the product backlog requires before the team start working on it.

. clear user stories written in the PBL

. Acceptance of what done might look like

. Estimated effor or complexity

. Agreement from the product owner that the PBL is ready


Defenition of Done ( DoD)

shared understanding and acceptance of what a completed increment of sprint end looks like and what is the criteria of acceptance to say that this product or sprint is done correctly was it tested ? was all the scenarios applied ?


. All code has been written and reviewed 
. All units have been written and passed
. All functunional and acceptance tests have been written and passed
. the product owner has reviewed and accepted the PBL
